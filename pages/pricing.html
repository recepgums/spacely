Frequently Asked Questions?

Is Spacely free to start?

Yes, you can sign up for the Starter plan and it's free forever! You can upgrade at anytime as your workflow and team grows.

What is Spacely cancellation policy?

If you cancel your subscription before the next renewal cycle, you will retain access to your account until the end of your subscription period.

How can I pay for Spacely?

You can pay for Spacely with any major credit card. For annual subscriptions, we can issue an invoice payable by check or wire transfer. Please contact us to arrange an invoice purchase.

How do I manage my subscription plan?

You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your plan anytime. When your subscription expires, your projects will remain intact, but you will lose access to them until your subscription is renewed.

Does Spacely have mobile and desktop apps?

Yes, you can download the Nifty app on iOS and Android. And, here are our Mac & Windows apps for desktop.

Is Spacely available in other languages?

While Spacely is currently only available in English, we have plans to integrate other languages soon. Let us know what language you’re missing.
